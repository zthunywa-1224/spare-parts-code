Our Story

Founded in 2012 in Durban, Spare Parts Hub began as a small garage‑side venture driven by a passion for keeping vehicles and machinery on the road. Over the past decade we’ve grown into a full‑service online retailer, but the core mission remains the same: to supply reliable, high‑quality components at fair prices, backed by the personal touch of a family‑run business.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is simple – to keep every machine running with the right part, at the right price, every time. We envision a future where customers across South Africa can instantly find the exact spare they need, supported by expert advice and rapid delivery, while we continue to expand our network of trusted manufacturers and sustainable practices.
